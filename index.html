Our School · Our Roots

Ladies of Porter.
Yesterday & today.

Archbishop Porter Girls' Senior High School stands on Fijai Hill — the Hill of Tranquility — overlooking Takoradi. Founded in 1965 by Archbishop John Kwadjo Amissah, it has shaped generations of brilliant, confident women.

We, the Class of 2001 — 2G1 Purple Divas, walked down that hill twenty-five years ago. Today we return not just to remember, but to give back to the school that made us.
